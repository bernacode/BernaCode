# Privacy Policy

**BernaCode** ("we", "us") operates as a software development and technology consulting agency. This policy details how we handle information on our corporate website.

## 1. Data Collection
Our website is primarily informational. However, we collect personal data when you voluntarily interact with us:

*   **Contact Form**: When requesting a quote or "Let's Talk", we collect your **name**, **email**, and **project details**.
*   **Purpose**: This information is used exclusively to:
    *   Evaluate the technical viability of your request.
    *   Schedule discovery meetings.
    *   Send commercial or legal proposals.

## 2. Tracking Technologies
**We do not use advertising trackers** or sell your information.
*   **Local Storage**: We use browser `localStorage` solely to remember your **language preference** (English/Spanish). This ensures consistent navigation across the site. This data never leaves your device.

## 3. Client Confidentiality
We understand that your project information can be sensitive.
*   Any technical or business detail shared via our form is treated with strict confidentiality.
*   We are willing to sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) before diving into operational details.

## 4. Rights
You can request the deletion of your contact history at any time by writing to [ceo@bernacode.com](mailto:ceo@bernacode.com).

*Last updated: January 2026. BernaCode reserves the right to update this policy as our services evolve.*
